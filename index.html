Kunstgreep '95 (KG95) is a space in The Hague and a community of creative makers engaged in contemporary discourses of art and design.

KG95 aims to produce a centre where radically imaginative, poetic, experimental, and socially situated practices can flourish and conversate with the city.

KG95 does this by hosting affordable studio spaces, shared facilities, and open events in the context of the residents' practices.

Since 1995, our building hosts 16 studios, a garden, an event space, and a rooftop. We are located in the city center, directly across from Brouwersgracht tram stop.
