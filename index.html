Call for new Members

From January 2026, a new studio space at KG95 ([sqm] - [$$$ p/m]) will be available in our building at Prinsegracht 64 in The Hague's city center, for one or more new members.

We are looking for professionally active contemporary artists based in The Hague and surroundings (Haaglanden) interested in joining our community. Our focus is on art-and-design practices that are experimental and/or socially-engaged. We are also seeking someone who is willing to contribute to the daily running of the initiative and to take on a role within the board.

KG95 (Kunstgreep'95) is an artists foundation established in 1995. Our building hosts 16 studios, a shared garden, and a few common facilities. We are located in the city center, directly across from Brouwersgracht tram stop.

Applications from all backgrounds and walks of life are welcome and encouraged!

If you are interested, please apply through this form: kg95.nl/apply

Deadline: December 12, 2025

For more information or questions, contact us at bestuur@kg95.nl with the subject line 

Open Call January 2026.
